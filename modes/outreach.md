# Mode: outreach — LinkedIn Outreach & Networking

Mode for generating personalized LinkedIn messages for recruiters, hiring managers, or team members. Triggered after an application is sent or to initiate discovery.

## Workflow

1. **Select Role**: If not provided, ask for the company/role from the tracker.
2. **Research Contact**: Use `web-search` to find the recruiter or hiring manager if name isn't provided.
3. **Generate Message**: Use the "I'm choosing you" tone and specific proof points from the evaluation report.

## Message Templates

### Type 1: Follow-up after applying
"Hi [Name], I just applied for the [Role] position. Given my background building [Specific Project/AI Assistant], I thought I'd reach out directly. I've spent the last few years [Result/Metric], and [Company]'s move towards [Vision/Product] is exactly why I applied. Looking forward to the next steps."

### Type 2: Discovery / Networking
"Hi [Name], I'm a [Current Title] specializing in [Niche]. I've been following [Company]'s work in [Specific Area] and wanted to connect. I recently built [Proof Point] and would love to chat about how your team is approaching [Challenge]."

## Tone Rules
- **Short**: Under 300 characters for connection requests.
- **Value-First**: Mention what you've BUILT, not just what you are.
- **Direct**: Clear call to action (connect or brief chat).
