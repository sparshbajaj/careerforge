# Mode: compare — Offer Comparison

Compare multiple job offers to help with prioritization or final decision making.

## Workflow

1. **Select Offers**: Compare the top 3-5 roles from the tracker or specific IDs provided by the user.
2. **Matrix Generation**: Create a comparison table.
3. **Synthesis**: Provide a stack-ranked recommendation based on candidate's priorities.

## Comparison Criteria

| Criteria | Importance |
|----------|------------|
| **Score** | Core match with profile |
| **Salary** | Total compensation estimate |
| **Growth** | Company stage and role impact |
| **Tech/AI** | Alignment with "Agentic/AI" career goal |
| **Location** | Remote/Hybrid preference |

## Output Format

- **Comparison Table**
- **Analysis of Trade-offs** (e.g., "Company A pays more, but Company B has a more advanced AI stack")
- **Final Verdict**
