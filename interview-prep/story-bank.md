# Story Bank — Master STAR+R Stories

This file accumulates your best interview stories over time. Each evaluation (Block F) adds new stories here. Instead of memorizing 100 answers, maintain 5-10 deep stories that you can bend to answer almost any behavioral question.

## How it works

1. Every time `/career-ops oferta` generates Block F (Interview Plan), new STAR+R stories get appended here
2. Before your next interview, review this file — your stories are already organized by theme
3. The "Big Three" questions can be answered with stories from this bank:
   - "Tell me about yourself" → combine 2-3 stories into a narrative
   - "Tell me about your most impactful project" → pick your highest-impact story
   - "Tell me about a conflict you resolved" → find a story with a Reflection

## Stories

### [AI / UX] Clara: Agentic AI Presence States
**Source:** Report #006 — Intercom — Senior Product Designer
**S (Situation):** At Memoria Call, we built "Clara," an AI assistant for meetings. Early data showed users were confused by the AI's lack of feedback—they didn't know when it was listening or if it had failed.
**T (Task):** Redesign the AI interaction model to build trust and increase adoption.
**A (Action):** Defined clear "presence states" (thinking, listening, taking notes) and created multi-turn correction patterns so users could easily fix AI mistakes.
**R (Result):** Reached 68% host adoption within 8 weeks and saw a 22% reduction in pre-join drop-off.
**Reflection:** I learned that AI trust isn't built on perfect accuracy, but on transparency and the user's ability to correct the system.
**Best for questions about:** AI interaction design, building trust, measuring impact.

### [Technical Design] Figma-to-React Bridge
**Source:** Report #006 — Intercom — Senior Product Designer
**S (Situation):** At SocialTalent, design-to-engineering handoff was a bottleneck. It took 2-3 days of manual work to translate Figma specs into production-ready components.
**T (Task):** Automate the handoff process to reduce friction and improve code accuracy.
**A (Action):** Developed a custom Figma plugin using the Figma API to automate semantic token renaming and generate React reference code from component specs.
**R (Result):** Reduced a 2-3 day manual process to under 4 minutes, cutting handoff overhead by ~20%.
**Reflection:** This reinforced my belief that the most effective designers are those who build tools to scale their own impact.
**Best for questions about:** Design systems, design-engineering collaboration, automation.
