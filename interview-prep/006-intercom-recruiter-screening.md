Intercom’s 'AI-first' mission with Fin is the perfect intersection of my experience in agentic UX and my technical craft."

## 💎 Key Stories (STAR+R)

### 1. The AI Specialist: Clara (Memoria Call)
*   **Context:** AI assistant for meetings.
*   **The Problem:** Users didn't trust the AI or know how to interact with an "agent."
*   **Action:** Defined "presence states" (thinking, listening, acting) and multi-turn correction patterns.
*   **Result:** 68% host adoption in 8 weeks; 15% increase in active users.
*   **Intercom Hook:** Connect this to **Fin AI**. You understand how to make AI feel human and reliable.

### 2. The Systems Builder: Figma-to-React Bridge (SocialTalent)
*   **Context:** Design-to-engineering handoff friction.
*   **The Problem:** Manual handoff was taking 2-3 days per component.
*   **Action:** Built a custom Figma plugin that generates React reference code from semantic tokens.
*   **Result:** Reduced process to under 4 minutes.
*   **Intercom Hook:** This proves you have the **"Builder Mindset"** and can speak the same language as their engineers.
